Random Mundane Magic Item Generator

This page provides a set of lists used to generate single-use mundane magical items for your fantasy roleplaying game of choice. You can roll on each list to generate an item at the table or you can print out the 1d100 magic item list and roll against that. Reloading this page will build a new list of 100 random magic items.

It's intended that the powers on these items are of a short duration, roughly five minutes, or in some cases can only be used once. The descriptions of these items are purposefully vague to give the GM the ability to customize and define them to fit the game. If you need further guidance on how these powers work, take a look at the d20 SRD spell descriptions that inspired many of these powers. Better yet, let you and the player discuss and decide how these powers work at the game.
